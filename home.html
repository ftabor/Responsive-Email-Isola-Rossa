Villa Leali
 
Located just 30 miles north of Rome in the region of Northern Lazio, the Leali estate offers one the rare opportunity to live in and amongst the history, architecture and culture of 13th century Italy.

The captivating Casa Vostra and its enchanting counterpart are at the very heart of the surrounding and endless 8 hectares of botanical gardens. Here one can witness some of the most beautiful scenery and explore the undulating lawns filled with fragrant roses and the forest of ancient umbrella pines. It is not then surprising that the grounds themselves are indeed responsible for recreating the magic of film - the movie "Silk, starring Keira Knightley, was filmed there.
